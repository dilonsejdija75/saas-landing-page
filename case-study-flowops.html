The Challenge

FlowOps, a workflow automation platform for marketing teams, faced a critical problem: users were taking too long to see value from the product. Key challenges included:

  • Average time to first automation: 14 days
  • 60% of users never created their first workflow
  • High churn rate in the first 30 days
  • Complex setup process deterring non-technical users

The Solution

FlowOps partnered with Boostly to create a streamlined onboarding experience focused on rapid value delivery:

1. Quick Win Strategy

Identified the simplest, highest-impact automation (email sequences) and guided all new users to create one within their first session.

2. Template-First Approach

Instead of starting from scratch, users were presented with pre-built templates relevant to their industry and role.

3. Smart Defaults

Pre-populated common settings and integrations based on the user's company profile and connected tools.

4. Contextual Assistance

Implemented just-in-time help that appeared exactly when users needed guidance, reducing friction without overwhelming them.

Implementation Process

Week 1: Analysis & Planning

Analyzed user behavior data to identify drop-off points and success patterns.

Week 2: Flow Design

Created new onboarding flows with Boostly's visual builder, focusing on the "aha moment" of creating the first automation.

Week 3: A/B Testing

Tested the new flow against the existing onboarding with 50% of new users.
